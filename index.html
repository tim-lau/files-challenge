Ash Ketchum

Image taken from the Nintendo Wikia

Ash Ketchum is a ten-year old Pokemon trainer. He roams the various regions with his trusty Pokemon and his friends, catching new Pokemon and collecting Gym Badges along the way. While he still has much to learn about being a trainer, the people around him admire his good character and determination. He dreams of one day becoming a Pokemon master.

The chosen one? I feel more like the frozen one.
